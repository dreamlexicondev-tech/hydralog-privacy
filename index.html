Privacy Policy for HydraLog

Last updated: March 8, 2026

HydraLog is a water reminder and hydration tracking application developed by DreamLexicon. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and what choices users have.

Information We Process

HydraLog stores hydration-related information locally on your device, such as water intake entries, reminder preferences, daily goal settings, sleep/wake schedule, language preference, and energy check-in data.

Local Storage

User data is primarily stored locally on the device using local app storage. HydraLog does not require users to create an account and does not operate its own backend server for account-based data collection.

Notifications

HydraLog uses local notifications to remind users to drink water. Notification permissions may be requested depending on the Android version used on the device.

Advertising

HydraLog may display advertisements using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain device identifiers, diagnostics, and usage-related data as described in their own policies.

Third-Party Services

HydraLog may use third-party SDKs including Google AdMob and other platform services required for notifications and app functionality.

Data Deletion

Users can clear certain app data from within the app settings. Depending on the feature, some preferences may remain unless the app is fully cleared or uninstalled.

Children’s Privacy

HydraLog is not specifically directed toward children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
