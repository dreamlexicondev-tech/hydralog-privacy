Privacy Policy for HydraLog

Last updated: March 8, 2026

HydraLog is a water reminder and hydration tracking mobile application developed by DreamLexicon. This Privacy Policy explains what information HydraLog processes, how that information is used, and what choices users have regarding their data.

1. Information HydraLog Processes

HydraLog is designed to work without requiring account creation or login. The app primarily stores and uses data locally on the user’s device to provide hydration tracking features.

Depending on how you use the app, HydraLog may process the following categories of information:

  • Water intake entries, including amount, date, time, and source of the entry (for example manual entry, widget action, or notification action)
  • Daily hydration goal settings
  • Wake time and sleep time preferences
  • Reminder settings and reminder schedules
  • Language and unit preferences
  • Energy or mood check-in data entered inside the app
  • Basic app preferences needed for features such as onboarding completion and app behavior

2. Local Storage

HydraLog primarily stores user data locally on the device using on-device app storage. HydraLog does not require a personal account and does not operate its own backend server for user accounts, cloud syncing, or profile-based data collection at this time.

This means your hydration logs, reminder settings, goals, and related app preferences are generally kept on your device, unless your device or third-party platform tools back them up independently.

3. Notifications and Reminder Features

HydraLog uses local notifications to send drink water reminders and reminder-related actions. Depending on your Android version, the app may request notification permission in order to deliver these reminders.

Notification actions may allow you to quickly interact with the app, such as adding a water intake amount, snoozing a reminder, or opening the app.

4. Home Screen Widgets

HydraLog may provide Android home screen widgets to let users quickly log water intake or view hydration progress. These widgets use locally available app data and device functionality to provide their features.

5. Advertising

HydraLog may display ads through third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob. These third-party services may collect or process certain information such as device identifiers, app interactions, diagnostics, and advertising-related data in accordance with their own privacy policies and platform requirements.

DreamLexicon does not directly control how third-party advertising providers process data on their own systems. Users should review the privacy policies of those providers for more information.

6. Third-Party Services

HydraLog may rely on third-party SDKs, libraries, and platform services in order to provide app functionality. These may include services related to:

  • Advertising
  • Local notifications
  • Device widgets
  • Basic app analytics, diagnostics, or platform-level service functionality provided by Android or integrated SDKs

7. Data Sharing

HydraLog does not sell your personal data. Data entered into the app is primarily stored locally on your device. However, third-party SDK providers such as advertising services may process certain technical or device-related data as part of their functionality.

8. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device unless it is deleted by you, cleared through app settings, removed through system-level app data clearing, or deleted when the app is uninstalled.

Depending on the current app version, some in-app data clearing tools may remove hydration and energy-related data while leaving certain preferences, reminders, or profile-related settings intact unless the app is fully cleared or uninstalled.

9. Data Deletion and User Choices

Users may have options within the app to clear certain locally stored data. Users may also remove app data using their device’s application settings or by uninstalling the app.

Users can also disable reminders, deny notification permissions, or limit ad personalization through their device or Google account settings where applicable.

10. Children’s Privacy

HydraLog is not specifically directed to children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided information in a way that raises concern, please contact us and we will review the issue.

11. Security

HydraLog uses reasonable measures appropriate to an app that primarily stores data locally on-device. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. International Users

If third-party service providers process technical or advertising-related data, that processing may occur in countries other than the one in which you are located, subject to the policies and infrastructure of those providers.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app updates, legal requirements, or service changes. The updated version will be posted at this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
